# Craft Codex — DE→EN Terminology Glossary

> Single source of truth for all English translations in this repo.
> Variant: **US English** (primary audience: Anthropic / international OSS community).
> Sources: Fine Woodworking glossary, Ian Kirby *The Complete Dovetail*, Lost Art Press,
> Wikipedia *Dovetail joint* (EN). Reviewed against actual usage in US/UK joinery literature.
>
> Rule: translations in code/content MUST use these exact forms. Legal corpus
> (`ris-corpus.ts`) is **never translated** — Austrian legal sources stay authentic German.
